# Feature Prioritization Framework

## Objective

Prevent scope creep.

---

## P0 Platform Critical

Examples:

* Asset Inventory
* Identity Mapping
* Graph Engine
* Risk Engine

Must ship before production.

---

## P1 Core Product

Examples:

* Dashboards
* Reports
* Monitoring
* Search

---

## P2 Enterprise Enhancements

Examples:

* SSO
* Multi-Tenant Support
* SIEM Integration

---

## P3 Strategic Future

Examples:

* Predictive Risk
* AI Insights
* Forecasting

---

## Scoring Model

Priority Score =

Business Value × User Impact × Strategic Value

divided by

Engineering Cost × Risk

---

Highest score wins.
