# Risk Engine

Version: 1.0

---

## Purpose

Prioritize attention based on context.

Not vulnerability counts.

---

## Risk Components

### Exposure

Can the asset be reached?

---

### Connectivity

How connected is it?

---

### Business Importance

How important is the asset?

---

### Confidence

How certain is the information?

---

### Change Frequency

How often does it change?

---

## Risk Formula

Risk Score =

Exposure
× Connectivity
× Importance
× Confidence

Adjusted by:

Change Multiplier

---

## Value Tiers

CRITICAL

HIGH

MEDIUM

LOW

---

## Risk Categories

0–25 = Low

26–50 = Moderate

51–75 = High

76–100 = Critical

---

## Risk Recalculation

Triggered by:

* New asset
* New relationship
* Configuration change
* Exposure change

---

## Explainability

Every score displays:

* Inputs
* Weighting
* Evidence

---

## Design Goal

Help users focus on what matters most.
